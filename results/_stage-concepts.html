CONCEPT BOARD

Four ways to break the monotone.

Same stage, four philosophies. The playing frequency stays the hero in all four.
A · THE SWEEP
Color lives in the transitions.
The stage stays pure while playing. The moment you switch stations, a four-color wave sweeps through and resolves into the new family (frozen mid-sweep here). Movement, not furniture.
B · THE HORIZON
One prism gradient, resting under the sound.
A soft four-color horizon lies along the stage floor, like light through a prism. The wave dances above it, never through it.
C · THE RICH VOICE
No foreign colors: the active family goes deep.
The wave itself becomes a living gradient of its own family, deep into bright, with a two-tone glow field. Monotone broken by depth, not by neighbors.
D · THE HALO FRAME
All four colors hold the player, none enter it.
The stage wears a fine four-color ring, the one place the whole spectrum touches the player. Inside stays pure for the playing frequency.
