Add Zodiac to your home screen

The Zodiac app is not on the app store (yet). It is what folks in the business call a "web app." It works just like an app-store app, but only once you add it to your home screen.

iPhone: open Safari (not Chrome), go to app.zodiac.fm, tap the share button (the box with the arrow up top), scroll down, tap "Add to Home Screen," then tap "Add."

Android: Chrome should offer to add it automatically. If not, tap the menu (3 dots, upper right) and tap "Add to home screen."

You now have a real functioning app. Sorry, we know it is a hassle. If you can't figure it out, please respond to this email and we will help you out.
