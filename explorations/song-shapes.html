Two answers, one screen

Every song a different shape. No play button.

Unique per song: each disc's shape is seeded from the song itself, so no two look alike, yet they're clearly one family. Skim the list, every song has its own face. (In production the seed is the song's real audio, the same idea as your cymatic tiles.)

No play button: the playing song is the one that's moving. Everything else is still. That motion is the signal, so the card stays clean. A tiny bars glyph confirms it.

Tap any song. It starts moving and the others go still, no play triangle needed. Tapping the whole card is play/pause.
