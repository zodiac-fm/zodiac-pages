The implication of choosing Core as the anchor: we keep Core where it is, then we lose the freedom to pick the other three by eye. They must land on Core's vividness ring, each in its own brightness lane, with only their hue free to roam. That one rule turns four loud strangers into one family that still reads as four. The hollow circles are those three colors, waiting to be derived.
