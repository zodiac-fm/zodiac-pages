Zodiac — gradient system

Three jobs, three gradient types. Verified clean (no mud), real colors.

1 · Functional (Primary → its Deep, same hue)

Lighting and depth on buttons, cards, active states. ~90% of the UI. Subtle, premium, predictable.

2 · Frequency identity (Primary → analogous neighbor)

Each frequency's own gradient. On tiles, the now-playing wave, the active frequency. This is how Love reads as Love.

3 · Brand halo (cross-frequency, expressive)

The high-impact Zodiac signature. Hero backgrounds, loading, app icon, marketing. Rare and deliberate, the visual bridge between brand pillars.
The rule: Functional + Frequency gradients ground the app (used constantly). The Brand halo is the "wow" wrapper, used sparingly on the most important moments. Neutrals still bind everything underneath.
