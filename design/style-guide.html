Neutrals

Each frequency: Tint (badge / surface backgrounds), Soft (hover, fills), Primary (the colour, buttons, labels), Deep (text on tint, pressed states), and the Gradient (now-playing, tiles, hero accents). Neutrals carry a faint warm/cool ground; Stage is the dark backdrop for the visualiser.
