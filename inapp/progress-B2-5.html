Noticing the Difference

Your four frequencies may be starting to meet you differently.

The same song may feel familiar with one frequency, neutral with another, or more intense and harder to place with another. The difference may show up through attention, preference, mood, energy, or what stays with you afterward.

Every response gives you something to compare. What matters now is noticing the contrast, even when it is subtle.

Try one familiar song with two frequencies and notice what changes.
