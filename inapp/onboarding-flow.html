How to listen

No headphones needed.

A decent speaker works just as well, a good Bluetooth speaker, your car, your AirPods. Just skip tinny phone and laptop speakers, they thin out the sound your frequencies ride on.

What to expect

Give it a few weeks to land.

Many people feel nothing at first, and that is completely normal. Feeling it is not the test. Most notice a shift around one month, a bigger one by three. It is working whether you notice it or not.
If it ever feels uncomfortable or stirs something up, that usually means it is working. Ease off, play the gentlest track for a few minutes a day, and build back up.
