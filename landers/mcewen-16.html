Frequently asked.

Do I need an exact birth time?

An exact time produces the most precise calculation. If you do not know it, Zodiac will guide you through the available options during setup.

Zodiac uses the astronomical positions tied to your birth moment to calculate frequency values. It is not a horoscope or personality reading. The output is music.

Do I need headphones?

No. Use headphones when they suit the moment, or play the music through speakers while you work, journal, meditate, or wind down.

Which frequency do I start with?

Start with the life area you want to support today: Core, Love, Vitality, or Abundance. Over time, keep all four in your listening mix.
